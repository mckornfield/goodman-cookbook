<!-- Ingredients -->

1 1/2 cups | strawberry jam
2 cups | buttermilk

<!-- split -->
<!-- Steps -->
* Stir ingredients together.
* Freeze until firm.
* Break up mixture and whip with mixer until light and fluffy.
* Refreeze.
