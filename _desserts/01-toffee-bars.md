<!-- Ingredients -->

1 cup | butter or margarine
1 cup | brown sugar
1 teaspoon | vanilla
2 cups | enriched flour, sifted
1 cup | semisweet chocolate pieces
1 cup | walnuts, chopped

<!-- split -->
